Audio DotA

Dota Audio is Finished!

This was a short project to allow Android users and people with limited bandwidth to hear all the action at The International 4.

Thanks so much for being a part of it.

About

Many DotA fans are bandwidth constrained, leading to the success of text-based commentary of The International 2014. Some members of the community have enough bandwidth to access audio streams, but Twitch does not offer audio-only streaming on PC and Android devices.

I've read many comments asking Twitch to support Audio only streams during TI4, and thought I could provide a temporary solution during this event. Hopefully, a more complete offering will be provided by Twitch soon. Until then, GL HF and enjoy the games!

Friends

If you're on a PC and looking to track stats and watch the game without wasting bandwidth or alerting your company, listen to Audio Dota while watching at Track Dota.

If you're on an android device, check out the DotA Companion App to get you all the stats about ongoing games without wasting your bandwidth.

Issues

Some people on android devices have been experiencing problems with the streams. While I'm finding a more permanent solution for these devices, there is a temporary fix that should work for today.

Download the VLC for Android Beta. Once it's installed, open the audio stream through VLC. The stream URL is http://dotaaudio.com:8000/DotaMultiMp3. For a full list of stream URLs, scroll down to the next section. If it's still not working, feel free to shoot me an email.
